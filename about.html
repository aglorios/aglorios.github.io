Maggie Brown

João Inada

João is a graduate of Columbia Journalism School 2014.
Originally from Brazil, he is a documentary filmmaker and multimedia reporter.
He was director of "Almost Tibetan", which was selected for the Asian American International Film Festival.
João also freelances for Al Jazeera as a video editor.

Alexandra Glorioso

Matteo Lonardi

Matteo is a graduate of Columbia Journalism School 2014.

Matt Yu

Matt Yu is a PhD candidate in the Electrical Engineering Department at Stanford University.
His primary research interests are computational photography, computer vision, and video streaming.
Most recently, his work has focused on developing systems for generating, delivering, and rendering cinematic virtual reality content.
